About Me

My background, education, and skills

I am a passionate Aerospace Engineering student pursuing a Bachelor’s degree at the University of California, Irvine. After graduating high school at the age of 15, I developed a strong sense of discipline and perseverance that continues to drive my pursuit of a career in the aerospace industry.

I have worked on a wide range of engineering projects involving data collection and analysis, system design, and hands-on assembly. In addition to personal projects, I currently serve as a subteam lead for two design teams at UCI, where I continue to grow as both an engineer and a leader.

Outside of coursework and engineering projects, I tutor high school students in physics and mathematics. I enjoy mentoring students and encouraging them to challenge themselves academically, including pursuing early graduation to gain a head start in their careers, as I did. I also launch High-Powered Model Rockets with the National Association of Rocketry (NAR) and am Level 1 NAR Certified.
